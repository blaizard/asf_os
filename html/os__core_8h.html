enum os_process_status

Status of the process.

Enumerator:
OS_PROCESS_IDLE 

The process is in idle state. It is not active nor pending, it is simply disabled and needs a manual action to bring it up.

OS_PROCESS_ACTIVE 

The process is active. It is stored in the active process list and will be proceed by the scheduler.

OS_PROCESS_PENDING 

The process is in pending mode. It is currently disabled, it does not appear in the active process list but is waiting for an event to trigger its re-activation.
